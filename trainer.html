// HOW TO USE

Match the moving image to the reference, then read off the couch shift.

A hidden 3D setup error is applied to today's image. Your job: slide and spin it until it lines up with the plan, then Check Match reads out the couch correction.

  • Cyan = reference — the planning DRR / CT (where things should be).
  • Orange = moving — today's portal / CBCT (what you are adjusting).
  • Two workflows: 2D/2D uses an AP + Lateral pair; CBCT uses three planes.
  • Between the views, all six axes are covered — align every view.
  • Finish with Check Match; green numbers mean you're within tolerance.
  • Clears, best times, XP & levels, daily streaks and achievements are tracked in Your Progress (start screen) and saved to your account.

  • Click a view to select it (gold outline); arrow keys then nudge it 0.5 mm (finer as you zoom).
  • Drag inside the red ring = move; drag outside = rotate (roll on AP, pitch on lateral).
  • Hold 1/2/3 + drag to lock one couch axis — 1 A‑P (drag ↕), 2 S‑I (drag ↕), 3 L‑R (drag ↔). Works from either view.
  • Each view has its own blend slider — fade cyan reference ↔ orange portal.
  • Tap Ctrl+A to flip the slider and flicker the two images so misalignment pops.
  • C Color Wash (aligned anatomy → white) · S Spyglass lens.
  • Prostate (kV fiducial) works differently — instead of overlaying a DRR you drag a 3‑marker triad onto the gold seeds in both views: plain drag moves all three (translation), Ctrl+drag moves the nearest marker (adds rotation). Check Match reads out the recovered couch shift.
  • Breast L · DIBH adds voice-coached breath-hold before the match — see the DIBH tab.

The Breast L · DIBH case adds a step before matching: you coach a deep-inspiration breath-hold by voice, and beam each field only while the patient holds inside the gating window.

  • Push-to-talk: hold the mic button or the Space bar to speak a command, release to send. A hands-free toggle keeps the mic open continuously.
  • Coaching cues: “breathe in” (deep breath & hold), “hold” (lock here), “a little more / a little less” (nudge into the gate), “breathe normally” (relax).
  • The amplitude trace rides green in-gate / amber out. The patient's natural breath usually lands outside the window — your job is to coach them in.
  • Beam On (button or Enter) only fires while in-gate and settled; it runs a short exposure the patient must hold through — a cough, drift, or relax interrupts it.
  • Each of the two breast fields is captured at its own breath-hold; once both are in, the portal unlocks and you match as usual.
  • Needs a microphone (Chrome/Edge). No mic? On-screen coaching buttons appear as a fallback.

Shortcuts work in both workflows unless tagged 2D or CBCT. Modifier keys match your device automatically — ⌘ / ⌥ on Mac, Ctrl / Alt on Windows & Linux.
