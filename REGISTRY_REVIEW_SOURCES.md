# Registry Review — Source Reference

> Companion to `REGISTRY_REVIEW_PLAN.md`. Every topic in the content blueprint (§2 of the plan) traces
> to a citation here. Organized as: **Tier 1** official standards & regulatory, **Tier 2** textbooks,
> then **per-subcategory** source lists grouping each topic to its corroborating reference.
>
> **Accuracy note:** secondary/exam-prep sites (Fiveable, Quizlet, weebly study pages, vendor blogs) are
> included because the research touched them, but they are **corroboration aids, not citations of record**.
> When authoring a question, cite the Tier-1 standard or Tier-2 textbook for the fact, not the prep site.
> The official ARRT spec PDF blocks automated fetches (HTTP 403) — it must be human-downloaded and diffed
> against the blueprint before any scored content goes live. See also the corrections in `REGISTRY_REVIEW_PLAN.md` §3.

## How to use this when authoring
1. Draft the item from the blueprint topic (`REGISTRY_REVIEW_PLAN.md` §2).
2. Pull the matching source here; **cite the Tier-1 standard or Tier-2 textbook** for the answer key.
3. Apply the §3 corrections (NRC dose limits, inverse-square notation, HDR/LDR isotopes, protocol-dependent
   dose numbers).
4. Store the chosen URL/citation in the question's `source` field in `review_data.js`.
5. Before the scored mock goes live: human-download the official ARRT spec PDF and diff against the blueprint.
