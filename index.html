// HOW TO USE

A random 3D offset is applied to the 2D portal images in both the AP/PA and lateral views. Align them to the reference DRRs, just like a real orthogonal-pair setup.

Each axis shows up where you'd expect: Lateral shifts the AP view sideways; Longitudinal moves both views up/down; Vertical shifts the lateral view front-to-back. Roll rotates the AP view in-plane; Pitch rotates the lateral view.

Click a view to select it (it gets a soft yellow outline), and the arrow keys then move that image along its visible directions. Each view is labelled at its edges: the AP/PA view shows SUP/INF and R/L (patient right on the viewer's left, per radiographic convention); the lateral view shows SUP/INF and ANT/POST. On AP: ←→ = right/left, ↑↓ = superior/inferior. On lateral: ←→ = anterior/posterior, ↑↓ = superior/inferior. Arrow-key nudges move 0.5 mm per press and automatically get finer as you zoom in (down to 0.1 mm), for precise alignment. Hold Ctrl + ←/→ to rotate the selected view in 0.1° steps — roll on the AP/PA, pitch on the lateral.

Each view has its own blend slider (in the bar below) to fade between reference and portal independently, then press Check Match. Press Ctrl+A to flip the selected view's slider to its mirror position (e.g. 80% → 20%) — tap it repeatedly to flicker between the two images and make misalignment jump out. [ and ] nudge the selected view's slider.

Drag to move: click and hold inside the red dotted ring and drag to slide that portal image into place. Drag outside the ring to rotate the image — roll on the AP/PA view, pitch on the lateral — spinning it around the isocenter like a rotation handle. Zoom: scroll wheel or +/- to zoom toward the cursor. When zoomed in, hold Shift and drag (or use the middle mouse button) to pan. Fullscreen: double-click a view (or press F); double-click again or press Esc to return.

Color Wash (C): tints the DRR orange and the portal blue. Where the two align, the colors combine toward white — misaligned anatomy shows orange/blue fringing. The blend slider biases emphasis between the orange and blue layers. Spyglass (S): a movable square lens that reveals the DRR over the portal. Click/drag to move it, drag the corner handle or scroll to resize, and use the blend slider to fade the DRR opacity inside the lens.

Keyboard: arrows = Lat/Lng · PgUp/PgDn = Vert · Q/E = roll · A/D = pitch · +/- = zoom · F = fullscreen · C = wash · S = spyglass · [ ] = blend · Ctrl+A = flip slider · R = reset · N = new
